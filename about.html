About Computer Knowledge

The Computer Knowledge is, put simply, an ongoing exploration about what, why and how. An observation on the binaries and the process of the programmable machines. In winter of 2010, when I was in RSMT, Varanasi I started writing about the basics to computers. Here the stuffs you can find is basically related to the bit world and global village.
